Photography narrative of a friend. This was the final project for the subject Photography and Composition from the University of Coimbra from Portugal.

The concept for this work relates to the life of a woman who feels insecure behind her makeup routine. I wanted to convey an unhappiness through the photos of a woman while putting on makeup contrasting with her happiness and freedom while wiping the makeup off her face.
