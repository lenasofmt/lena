This work began with finding several typography examples from the downtown of Coimbra and then making my own typography with different modules of each existing typography.

So, first things first I went to the downtown of Coimbra and started looking for nice typographies

After gathering a lot of inspiration, I dedicated myself to doing my own typography through modules taken from my favorite 3 typographies from downtown Coimbra. This is the result!
